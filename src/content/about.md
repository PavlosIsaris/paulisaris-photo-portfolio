### Bio

Paul Isaris was born in Athens. From an early age, he felt a deep pull toward the arts, which for him are not only means of expression, but also bridges of communication between the inner and outer worlds.

Through art, he seeks ways to give form to the unspoken.<br>
Photography, writing, and music intertwine into a single language.

Each of his works is an invitation to see, to listen, and to feel beyond the surface.
